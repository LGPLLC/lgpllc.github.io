Overview

Custom replacement dash cover designed for the Tigé R22 series of boats (2016–2020). Built for durability, precision, and aesthetic refinement, this cover protects dash electronics while enhancing the look of the helm. Each piece is engineered to OEM geometry and reinforced for marine use.

Customization & Model Options

This design can be adapted to match your preferred look and functionality. Three configurations are currently in development:

  • Eyebrow Style – a low-profile shroud emphasizing screen visibility
  • Left-Opening Lid – quick side-access for electronics and storage
  • Vertical-Opening Lid – traditional hinged cover for full-face protection

Custom orders can include surface texture, logo placement, color changes, and latch or hinge upgrades.

Fulfillment Time: 1–3 weeks (depending on customization).
Note: Price does not include shipping.

Model Fitment

Compatible with Tigé R22 and R22-SK (Model Years 2016–2020).
Designed to match OEM console contours and standard CLEAR or analog-style display layouts.

Disclaimer: Verify your console shape and screen cutout dimensions before ordering. Slight variations may exist between production years or dealer-installed helm options.

Material & Build

  • Marine-grade ABS/ASA Blend – UV and heat resistant
  • Optional Textured or Smooth Finish
  • Custom latch system for secure and serviceable access
