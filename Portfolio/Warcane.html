War Cane

A robust, multi-use walking cane with modular attachments and durable construction.

Specifications

  • Material: 6061 aluminum shaft, stainless fittings
  • Length: Adjustable 36–42 in (custom sizes available)
  • Finish: Powder coat or polished raw
  • Weight capacity: tested to 300 lb
  • Typical turnaround: 1–3 weeks depending on options
Order Request Quote

Details

The War Cane is designed for both mobility and defense utility. It features a modular tip that accepts spares and a removable pommel for storage.

Available options

  • Custom lengths and grip materials (leather, rubber, or wood)
  • Optional forged pommel or decorative collar
  • Color/finish choices
