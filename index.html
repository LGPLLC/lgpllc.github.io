Mission

Our mission is to serve our customers with innovative design and fabrication solutions that empower creativity, self-reliance, and technical craftsmanship.

About the Founder

L.G.P. LLC was founded by a Marine Corps veteran and E.I.T. (Engineer-in-Training) to combine advanced design and fabrication into practical, real-world solutions. The company operates from Sparks, NV.

Capabilities & Services

  • CAD Modeling & Product Design — Custom modeling, reverse engineering, and visualization.
  • Large-Format Additive Manufacturing — Modix 180X-scale printing for prototypes and automotive builds.
  • Metal fabrication and 3-D printing — build for your use case and easy assembly.
  • Custom Fabrication & Finishing — Machining, welding, and metalworking for functional prototypes.
  • Solution Hosting & Licensing — Proprietary L.G.P. designs available for partners and resale.
  • Veteran Network (In Development) — A scalable system for veteran-hosted fabrication cells.
